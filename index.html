A Superior Sip

Calm Energy, Clear Focus.

While coffee offers a jarring spike of energy, matcha provides a state of calm alertness. This is thanks to L-theanine, a unique amino acid that works in synergy with caffeine. It smooths out the energy release, fostering sustained focus and mental clarity without the anxiety, jitters, or the inevitable afternoon crash associated with other caffeinated drinks. It's not just energy; it's balanced, mindful vitality.

The Mark of Excellence

From Ancient Rituals to Modern Kitchens.

We offer three distinct grades of matcha, each tailored for a specific purpose, ensuring the perfect experience every time.

Ceremonial Grade

Our highest grade, reserved for traditional preparation. It boasts a brilliant, vibrant green hue and a complex, umami-rich flavour profile with a smooth, creamy finish and no bitterness. This is matcha in its purest form.

Premium Grade

A versatile, high-quality choice perfect for daily drinking, lattes, and premium beverages. It maintains a beautiful green color and a robust flavour that is bold yet smooth, with minimal astringency.

Culinary Grade

Specially blended for cooking and baking. Its stronger, more astringent flavour profile shines through in recipes, from smoothies and desserts to sauces. It imparts a rich green color and a distinct matcha taste to your creations.

Mindful Consumption

The Green Wave in India.

A Health-Conscious Shift

Across India's bustling cities, from Bengaluru to Mumbai, a quiet revolution is brewing. Health-conscious consumers are moving beyond transient fads and embracing mindful wellness practices. Matcha, with its deep cultural roots and scientifically-backed benefits, has become the beverage of choice for those seeking both vitality and tranquility in their daily lives.

Less Waste, More Goodness

This shift is also an environmentally conscious one. Unlike coffee, where grounds are discarded, or tea, which often comes in single-use bags, matcha utilizes the entire tea leaf. The leaves are stone-ground into a fine powder and consumed whole, leading to maximum nutrient absorption and minimal waste. It's a simple, elegant practice that is as kind to the planet as it is to your body.
